Volunteers and cheers

Without your support, the Navy Ten-Miler would not be possible. Our course is spectator-friendly – so come out and let our participants know they are running with purpose.

You don’t have to run to show your support; Volunteer! Help us deliver the best experience for our participants. Volunteers will contribute to a larger cause and receive a free t-shirt. Every single volunteer will also be listed in our annual race recap message.
